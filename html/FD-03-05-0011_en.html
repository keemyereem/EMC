Social Contribution

The beginning of story

Environment Management Corporation has initiated various CSR activities such as ‘Blanket Wash’ and ‘Insulation Cap’(ppyokppyoki in Korean) based on the motif of ‘cleanness’ and ‘warmth’ in order to establish lasting bonds with socially disadvantaged classes such as elderly people living alone and children that are forced to become heads of households at a young age. We are writing this ‘Happy Virus Story’ every day with the hope of spreading happiness and warmth all over the country. Executives of Environment Management Corporation take pride in their stewardship of the protection of the environment and actively participate in various CSR activities.

Mission ‘Good company’ growing together with our community through sharing

Main Activities

  • ‘Helping neighbors in need’ Blanket Wash We visit socially disadvantaged single-senior and child-head households in the spring and summer and wash their bulky blankets.

  • ‘Warmth’ Air cap to keep warm We share warmth in the winter season by installing insulation air caps at single-senior households.

  • ‘Water’ River cleaning We regularly clean rivers and streams near sewage treatment plants and transform them into eco parks and community shelters.

  • ‘Food’ Provide meals We regularly visit community welfare centers and provide meals to the needy.

  • ‘Little big heart(小小大心)’ campaign We run ‘Little big heart’ campaign to deliver a big heart while it’s a little money from our employee’s voluntary money collection from their salary every month. We send our warmth to people in need every end of year through a matching grant that the company matches up the amount employees donate.
